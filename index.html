// Coaching Intelligence

NON-NEGOTIABLE
RULES

01
Protein First, Always

Hit 200g protein every single day without exception. This is what prevents muscle loss during aggressive deficit. No excuses.

02
Flexibility is Mandatory

Every session ends with flexibility work. No skipping. Tight muscles = reduced performance = injury = missed training days = failed program.

03
Carbs are a Tool

Keep net carbs under 60g on rest days. On training days, add 40–60g carbs around the workout window only. Carb timing matters.

04
Sleep 8 Hours

Sleep is where fat loss hormones reset. Ghrelin spikes when sleep-deprived. 8h minimum or the program fails despite perfect training.

05
Track Everything

Weigh daily, track weekly average. Log food for at least the first 4 weeks until macro targets are memorized. What gets measured gets managed.

06
Deloads are Sacred

Weeks 4 and 8 are deload weeks. Reduce training volume 30%. Maintain diet strictly. This is where body recomposition consolidates.
